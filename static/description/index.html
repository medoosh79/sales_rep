Sales Representative Management PRO

🚀 Key Features

👥 Sales Representative Management

Complete management of sales representatives with advanced assignment capabilities, performance tracking, and territory management.

🗺️ Geo Segmentation & Territory Assignment

Advanced geographical segmentation with interactive maps, territory assignment, and location-based analytics.

📊 Advanced Analytics Dashboard

Real-time analytics with interactive charts, performance metrics, and comprehensive reporting capabilities.

📱 Daily Visit Tracking

Complete visit scheduling, tracking, and reporting with GPS integration and real-time updates.

💰 Commission Management

Automated commission calculations, tracking, and reporting with flexible commission structures.

📈 Performance Analytics

Comprehensive performance tracking with KPI monitoring, goal setting, and achievement analysis.

Sales Management Territory Management Analytics GPS Tracking Commission Management Performance Tracking Mobile Ready Enterprise

📋 Requirements

  • Odoo 18.0 Enterprise Edition
  • Python packages: reportlab, xlsxwriter, geopy, folium
  • Base modules: sale, sale_management, mail, hr, web, portal

💡 Installation

  1. Download and extract the module to your custom addons directory
  2. Update the addons list in Odoo
  3. Install the module from Apps menu
  4. Configure your sales representatives and territories
